## Sourcing Recommendations

### Steel Suppliers
- **Local Steel Service Centers:** Often best prices for cut-to-length
- **Metal Supermarkets:** Convenient but higher prices
- **Online:** TMS Steel, Online Metals (shipping can be expensive)

### Hydraulics
- **Surplus Center:** Great prices on hydraulic components
- **Northern Tool:** Good selection, competitive pricing
- **Graingers:** Industrial quality, higher prices

### Fasteners
- **Fastenal:** Wide selection, local branches
- **McMaster-Carr:** Excellent catalog, fast shipping
- **Local hardware store:** Convenient for small quantities

### Electronics
- **Arduino Store:** Official source for controllers
- **DigiKey / Mouser:** Electronic components
- **SparkFun:** Sensors and development boards
